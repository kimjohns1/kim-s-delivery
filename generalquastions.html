Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to the most common questions about our services

Delivery Services

What areas do you deliver to?

We provide delivery services throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. For same-day delivery, we cover a 50-mile radius from our main hub. Express shipping is available nationwide with 1-3 business day delivery times.

How fast is same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery typically takes 2-6 hours depending on your location and the time of day you place your order. Orders placed before 2 PM are guaranteed same-day delivery, while orders after 2 PM may be delivered the next business day.

Can I track my package?

Yes! All packages come with real-time tracking. You'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS once your package is picked up. You can track your delivery progress on our website or mobile app.

What size packages can you handle?

We handle packages from small envelopes to large items up to 150 lbs. Maximum dimensions are 48" x 36" x 36". For oversized or specialty items, please contact us for a custom quote and handling arrangements.

Pricing & Billing

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on package size, weight, delivery distance, and service type. Same-day delivery starts at $15, express shipping starts at $8, and standard delivery starts at $5. Use our online calculator for an instant quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and for business accounts, we offer net-30 billing terms with approved credit.

Are there any additional fees?

Our quoted prices include all standard fees. Additional charges may apply for special handling (fragile items), weekend delivery, or delivery to restricted access areas. All fees are clearly disclosed before you confirm your order.

Policies & Security

What if my package is damaged or lost?

All packages are insured up to $100 at no extra cost. For higher-value items, additional insurance is available. If your package is damaged or lost, we'll investigate immediately and provide full compensation according to our insurance policy.

How secure are my packages?

We use GPS tracking on all vehicles, secure handling protocols, and require signature confirmation for deliveries over $50. Our drivers are background-checked and trained in secure package handling procedures.

Can I cancel or modify my delivery?

You can cancel or modify your delivery up to 1 hour after booking for same-day service, or up to 24 hours for express shipping. Cancellations made within the allowed timeframe receive a full refund minus a small processing fee.

What items cannot be shipped?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, perishable food items, live animals, illegal substances, or items valued over $5,000 without special arrangements. Contact us if you're unsure about a specific item.
