Our Story

Founded in 2018, Kim's Delivery started with a simple mission: to provide reliable, fast, and affordable delivery services to our community. What began as a small local operation has grown into a trusted regional delivery network.

Today, we serve over 10,000 customers annually, handling everything from urgent same-day deliveries to complex logistics solutions. Our success is built on three core principles: reliability, transparency, and exceptional customer service.
