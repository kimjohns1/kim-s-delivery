About TechStart Solutions

TechStart Solutions is a rapidly growing e-commerce platform specializing in innovative tech accessories and gadgets. Founded in 2020, the company experienced explosive growth during the pandemic, scaling from 100 orders per month to over 5,000 orders monthly by 2023.

As their business expanded, TechStart faced significant challenges with their existing delivery infrastructure, leading to delayed shipments, increased costs, and declining customer satisfaction scores.

The Challenge

TechStart Solutions was struggling with multiple delivery-related issues that were impacting their growth and customer retention

Inconsistent Delivery Times

Limited Market Reach

Expansion plans were stalled due to unreliable delivery partners in new geographic markets.
