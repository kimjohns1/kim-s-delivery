Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What are your delivery hours?

We offer 24/7 delivery services for urgent packages. Regular deliveries are made Monday through Saturday, 8AM to 8PM.

How can I track my package?

You'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS once your package is picked up. Use this number on our website or call our support line.

Do you offer insurance for valuable items?

Yes, we provide comprehensive insurance coverage for valuable and fragile items. Contact us for specific coverage details and rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, mobile money, and bank transfers. Corporate accounts can also be set up for regular customers.
