Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our delivery plans

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my delivery limit?

For Basic and Professional plans, additional deliveries are charged at our standard per-delivery rate. Enterprise plans include unlimited deliveries.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Our Enterprise plan includes custom logistics solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly subscription fee and any additional delivery charges if applicable.
