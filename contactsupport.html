Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our delivery services

How can I track my package?

You can track your package using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Visit our tracking page or call our support line for real-time updates.

What are your delivery hours?

We offer deliveries Monday through Saturday from 8 AM to 8 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM on weekdays.

Do you provide insurance for packages?

Yes, we offer comprehensive insurance coverage for all packages. Coverage options vary based on package value and delivery type. Contact us for specific insurance details.

What if my package is damaged or lost?

We take full responsibility for packages in our care. If your package is damaged or lost, contact us immediately and we'll investigate and provide compensation according to our insurance policy.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Address changes are possible if the package hasn't been dispatched. Contact our support team as soon as possible to modify your delivery address.
